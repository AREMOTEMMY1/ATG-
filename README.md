# **ATG GROUP — Corporate Profile**

## **About ATG GROUP**
ATG GROUP is a global ecosystem operating at the intersection of logistics, finance, technology, and trade. Built on a foundation of intelligence and precision, ATG GROUP unifies multiple companies, platforms, and capabilities into one integrated infrastructure for the movement of goods, money, and data across borders.

The Group combines advanced tracking systems, financial engines, digital platforms, and trade operations into a single, cohesive network — enabling global visibility, operational efficiency, and real‑time intelligence at scale.
